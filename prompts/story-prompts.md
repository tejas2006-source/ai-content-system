# Story Prompts

## Format

Each story short should include:
- A strong hook in the first 2 seconds
- A fast-moving mini plot
- A twist ending
- A call to follow for part 2

## Starter prompts

1. A broke young hustler in a Vice City-style world gets offered a mysterious late-night job that looks easy at first but turns dangerous fast.

2. A character finds a luxury car with keys inside and thinks it is his lucky day, but the boot contains something unexpected.

3. Two friends try to make fast money in a neon-lit city by accepting a shady delivery job, then realize they are being watched.

4. A low-level street hustler pretends to be rich for one night and accidentally gets invited into a dangerous opportunity.

5. A character receives a text promising easy cash if he reaches a nightclub in ten minutes, but the deal is not what it seems.

## Style notes

- High energy
- Cinematic
- Slightly comedic
- Tension and suspense
- Strong cliffhangers
- Designed for 30 to 45 second vertical videos
